Specialized training in surgical assistance, sterilization protocols, and anesthesia equipment management for clinical excellence.

Program Overview

Surgical & Anesthesia Excellence

B.Sc. Operation Theatre & Anesthesia Technology is a specialized branch of Allied Health Sciences that focuses on preparing students to manage the operating theatre and assist in anesthesia administration.

The program provides comprehensive training in preparing surgical environments, mastering surgical instrumentation, and managing anesthesia equipment to ensure patient safety and procedure success.

Key Details

  • Course Duration: 4 Years (3 years academic + 1 year internship)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB)
  • Degree Status: B.Sc. Allied Health Science

Career Opportunities

Graduates find diverse roles in operating theatres, emergency departments, and specialized surgical centers.

OT Technologist

Ensuring the seamless operation of surgical suites and providing advanced assistance to surgical teams.

Anesthesia Technician

Managing anesthesia equipment and monitoring patient vitals during complex surgical procedures.

CSSD In-charge

Overseeing sterilization protocols and managing the distribution of surgical instruments.
