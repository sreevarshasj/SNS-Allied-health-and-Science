Academic Programs

Specially Curated Industry Standard B.Sc. Programs

Excellence in Health Science Education

World-class curriculum enhanced with design thinking, clinical excellence, and innovation in health science education.

Cardio Pulmonary Perfusion Care

4-Year Degree Program

Specialized training in heart-lung machines and supporting patients during cardiac surgery.

OT & Anesthesia Technology

4-Year Degree Program

Specialized training in surgical assistance, sterilization, and anesthesia equipment management.

Physician Assistant

4-Year Degree Program

Comprehensive clinical practice training to support physicians in diverse medical environments.

Radiography & Imaging

4-Year Degree Program

Training in medical imaging technologies including X-Ray, CT, and MRI for accurate diagnosis.

Cardiac Technology

4-Year Degree Program

Specialized training in invasive and non-invasive cardiovascular procedures and diagnosis.

Dialysis Therapy Technology

4-Year Degree Program

Specialized training in renal replacement therapy and management of patients with kidney failure.

Medical Lab Technology

4-Year Degree Program

Training in clinical laboratory procedures for diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.

Optometry

4-Year Degree Program

Professional training in eye care, vision science, and examination technologies.

Respiratory Therapy

4-Year Degree Program

Expertise in pulmonary medicine and managing patients with respiratory disorders.
